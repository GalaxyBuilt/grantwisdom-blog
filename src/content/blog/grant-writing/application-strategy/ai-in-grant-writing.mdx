Finding funding for your project or organization can feel like searching for a needle in a haystack. With thousands of opportunities available, where do you even start?

## 1. Define Your Needs

Before you start searching, clearly define what you need funding for. Is it for a specific project, operational costs, or research? Having a clear goal will help you filter out irrelevant opportunities.

## 2. Research Eligibility

There is nothing worse than spending hours on an application only to realize you are not eligible. Pay close attention to geographic restrictions, organization type requirements, and industry focus.

## 3. Use the Right Tools

Platforms like GrantWisdom centralize thousands of grants, making it easier to filter by criteria that matter to you. Use these tools to save time and discover opportunities you might have missed.
