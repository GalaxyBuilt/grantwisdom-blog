2026 is shaping up to be a significant year for nonprofit funding. From climate initiatives to social justice programs, several major foundations have announced record-breaking grant pools.

## Focus on Sustainability

Many of this year's top grants are focused on long-term sustainability and systemic change rather than short-term projects. Foundations are looking for organizations that have a clear roadmap for the next 5-10 years.

## Community-Led Initiatives

We are seeing a shift towards funding community-led grassroots organizations. Demonstrating strong community ties and local impact is more important than ever.
