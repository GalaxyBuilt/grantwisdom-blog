The landscape of grant writing has changed dramatically over the last year. What used to take weeks of drafting and refining can now be accomplished in a fraction of the time with the help of artificial intelligence.

## Precision and Personalization

Modern AI tools don't just generate generic text. They can analyze your organization's history, the specific requirements of a grant, and past winning proposals to draft highly personalized content.

## Efficiency at Scale

For organizations applying to multiple grants, AI allows for rapid scaling of the application process without sacrificing quality. This means more shots on goal and a higher chance of securing funding.
